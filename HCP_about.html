About Us

Our Origin

Higher Circuit Professionals was founded in 2019 as a subsidiary of an Austrian enterprise, with a vision to transform the African trade landscape. Fully motivated by its parent company, they focus on developing specialized trade skills through it Electroskills Lab. The company was founded by a team of vibrant individuals, who share a passion for driving growth and excellence across Africa. By empowering the young and talented, Higher Circuit Professionals is paving the way for a self-sufficient future, fostering economic and technical development. The company is committed to promoting self-reliance and reducing Africa's dependence on foreign experts by nurturing local talent and fostering sustainable growth. Also, they offer electrical services and general contracting in electrical and electronics, delivering comprehensive solutions to meet diverse industry needs.
